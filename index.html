Investment approach

How I build a thesis.

Every model on this page followed the same five-step process. Predictable process is how individual calls scale into a repeatable track record.

1

Source

Screen the universe on margin structure, FCF yield, and quality metrics. Watch sector rotation, earnings revisions, and insider activity for entry signals.

Bloomberg EQS · COMB · RV
2

Prime

Map the competitive landscape, TAM, and cyclicality. Read the last 5+ years of 10-Ks, earnings transcripts, and primary industry data before I touch Excel.

10-K · Transcripts · EDGAR
3

Model

Integrated IS/BS/CF with segment-level operating drivers. DCF, trading comps, precedent transactions, and LBO fundamentals triangulate a fair-value range.

Excel · DCF · Comps · Precedents
4

Stress-test

Two-variable sensitivity on WACC and terminal growth; bear/base/bull scenario tables on revenue growth, margin, and exit multiple. If the call survives the bear case, it's a call.

Sensitivity · Scenarios
5

Pitch & monitor

Written memo and verbal pitch to the 10-member investment committee. Post-pitch: monitor earnings, guidance, and macro; update the model and re-price the thesis.

IC memo · Earnings · Bloomberg ERN
